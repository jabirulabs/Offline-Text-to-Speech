# Privacy Policy for Hannah AI Voice Generation (Offline Text to Speech)

**Effective Date:** May 22, 2026

Thank you for choosing to use Hannah AI Voice Generation ("the App"). This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how your data is handled when you use our application. Because our app is built with a focus on privacy and offline processing, our data collection is minimal to non-existent.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage

### Offline Processing (Text to Speech)
Hannah AI Voice Generation is an offline-first application. **We do not collect, store, transmit, or share the text you type into the app.** All text-to-speech (TTS) processing is performed locally on your device using on-device Machine Learning models. Your text input and the generated audio never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to share the audio files yourself.

### Internet Access
The App requests "Internet" permission. This is used strictly for:
* Downloading the necessary voice models (AI models) to your device upon first launch so the app can function entirely offline afterward.
* (If applicable) Fetching non-intrusive advertisements or crash analytics to improve app stability. 

### Storage Permissions
The App requests Write External Storage permission (`WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` on devices running Android 10 or lower).
* **Why it is needed:** This permission (on older Android versions) and the system MediaStore API are used solely to save your generated audio files (`.wav` files) to your device's public Music folder so you can access them outside the app.
* **How it is used:** We do not scan, collect, or read any personal files, photos, or documents on your device. We only access the private app folder and the audio files generated by Hannah AI Voice Generation.

## 2. Third-Party Services
While the core functionality of the App operates offline, we may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you, such as Google Play Services (for app distribution and updates) or crash reporting tools (like Firebase Crashlytics) to fix bugs. 
These third-party services have their own Privacy Policies regarding how they handle data.

## 3. Data Security
We value your trust in providing us with your personal information, thus we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. Since the app functions primarily offline, your typed text and generated audio are as secure as the physical security of your device. We recommend keeping your device locked with a PIN or biometrics.

## 4. Children’s Privacy
This App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. Since the app operates offline and does not collect personal data, children can safely use the app to generate speech locally.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted.
